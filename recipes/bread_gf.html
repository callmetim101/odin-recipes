Gluten Free Bread

Like most people with celiac disease, I find white bread is one of the foods our gluten-free family misses the most. After much trial and error in making gluten-free bread taste like real white bread, I created this wonderful gluten-free white bread recipe.

Steps

  1. 3 tablespoons white sugar
  2. 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  3. 1 ¼ cups warm water (about 110 degrees F/43 degrees C)
  4. 1 ⅓ cups rice flour
  5. ½ cup potato starch
  6. ½ cup cornstarch
  7. ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  8. 3 eggs
  9. 1 tablespoon xanthan gum
  10. 1 ½ teaspoons salt
